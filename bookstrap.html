Here's a time line of Mark Elliot Zuckerberg's life

  • 1914 - Born in White Plains, New York, U.S.
  • 2004 - Zuckerberg launched Facebook from his Harvard dormitory room
  • 2010 - Time magazine has named Zuckerberg among the 100 wealthiest and most influential people in the world as a part of its Person of the Year distinction.
  • 2012 -Facebook expanded rapidly, with one billion users
  • 2016 - Zuckerberg was ranked 10th on Forbes list of The World's Most Powerful People.
